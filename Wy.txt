        Relations entre W(x) et y(x)
------------------------------------------------------------
y(x) = ln(x)/W(ln(x))  <==>  W(ln(x)) = ln(x)/y(x)
Soit : a = ln(x)  <==>  x = e^a 
W(a) = ln(x)/y(x) = a/y(e^a)  <==>  W(x) = x/y(e^x)
------------------------------------------------------------
Si : y = f(x) = x^x  <==>  ln(y) = x*ln(x)  
en fonction inverse : ln(x) = y*ln(y)
Soit w = ln(y)  <==>  y = e^w
ln(x) = y*ln(y) = w*e^w  <==> w = W(ln(x))
=> y = e^w = e^W(ln(x))  <==>  W(ln(x)) = ln(y(x))
W(a) = ln(y(e^a))  <==>  W(x) = ln(y(e^x))
------------------------------------------------------------
=> : y(x) = ln(x)/W(ln(x)) = e^W(ln(x))
et : W(x) = x/y(e^x) = ln(y(e^x))
------------------------------------------------------------
